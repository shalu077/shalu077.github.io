    Welcome To Shalu chisti Makeup Artist 

Hi I’m Shalu Chisti ! I love people and people love to feel beautiful, which is the reason I’ve spent last 10 years engulfed in doing makeup. Is makeup the best way to gain self confidence? No way! But it can help a little, plus it’s FUN! So I hope I can help your life be a little bit happier, and perhaps more colorful.
